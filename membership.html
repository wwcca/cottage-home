When you join the WWCCA, you have representation at various meetings related to the local roads, water management plans, etc. You will receive a newsletter at least once a year, and would have voting privileges at the Annual General Meeting and a voice among cottage owners with concerns similar to yours. Your membership fee, just $25 per cottage property assists with ongoing WWCCA initiatives such as this website, cottage watch program, etc.

To become a member of the Watabeag-Wildgoose Community Cottager's Association please complete the membership form and send to the address identified on the form (with payment). 

New Members - if you know of a new cottager to the lake please feel free point them over to this website!

Privacy Policy - the WWCCA privacy policy is available upon request.
